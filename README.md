# EmporiUm Sales Territory Analysis
### Eyerusalem Debero | Year Up United | Data Analyst | Dallas Texas | Capstone 2

## Project Description
This project analyzes 4 years of in-store sales data for EmporiUm,
a growing student bookstore chain that sells technology gear, textbooks,
art supplies, and more. The analysis compares two sales territories
managed by Erbayne Middleton (Maine) and Shruti Reddy (Maryland).

## Territories Analyzed
- **Erbayne Middleton** — 6 stores in Maine (Northeast Region)
- **Shruti Reddy** — 9 stores in Maryland (East Region)
- **Data Period** — January 2022 to December 2025
- **Total Transactions Analyzed** — 100,000+

## What This Project Covers
- Monthly and yearly revenue trends
- Store performance rankings
- Top customer analysis using rewards data
- Product category and subcategory performance
- Revenue dip investigation — seasonal vs operational
- Marketing recommendations for Q1 next quarter
- Bonus analysis — 15 additional business questions

## Key Findings
- Technology and Accessories drives 70% of revenue in both territories
- Erbayne Middleton grew 4x from 2022 to 2025 (400% growth)
- North Harford store generates 75% of Shruti Reddy's territory revenue
- Trent Lane (ID #99) is a top 10 customer in BOTH territories
